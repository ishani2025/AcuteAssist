Chunk 1
Clinical Context:
Coronary artery disease occurs when plaque builds up inside coronary arteries, narrowing them and reducing blood flow to the heart muscle.

Doctor Action Steps:
1. Assess patient symptoms such as chest pain or shortness of breath.
2. Evaluate cardiovascular risk factors.
3. Order ECG and cardiac biomarkers.
4. Initiate risk factor management.
5. Monitor patient for worsening symptoms.

Emergency Level: MODERATE
